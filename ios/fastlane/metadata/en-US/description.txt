LingoPlay is a gamified learning app that makes studying languages and school subjects actually fun.

Learn 12 languages including Spanish, French, Japanese, Arabic, and more — with vocabulary drills, spaced repetition, and speech recognition so you practice real pronunciation.

Go beyond languages with courses in programming, math, science, music theory, chess, and history. School students can tackle curriculum-aligned content for courses like Pre-Calculus and Anatomy.

Features:
• Skill tree progression — unlock new topics as you advance
• Spaced repetition review — surface cards right when you're about to forget them
• Speech recognition — practice speaking, not just reading
• Achievements and streaks — stay motivated with milestones
• Light and dark mode
• No account required — all progress is stored on your device

LingoPlay works offline. No subscription. No ads. Just learning.
